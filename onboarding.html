Let's Get You Started

Complete these steps to unlock the full ZimCrowd experience

1. Verify Your Identity

Upload your National ID and complete KYC verification for security and compliance

2. Complete Your Profile

Add personal information, employment details, and contact information

3. Link Payment Method

4. Credit Assessment

Submit bank statements for automated risk scoring and personalized loan offers
