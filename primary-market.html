How the Primary Market Works

Simple steps to start funding community loans and earning returns on your investment.

01

Browse & Select

+

Use our filters to find loan requests that match your investment criteria. Review borrower profiles, risk assessments, and loan details to make informed decisions.

02

Fund Portions

+

Choose how much you want to invest in each loan. You can fund a portion of a larger loan or the entire amount of smaller requests. Diversify your portfolio across multiple borrowers.

03

Earn Returns

+

Borrowers repay their loans according to agreed terms. You receive your principal plus interest directly through the platform. Track your investments and returns in real-time.

04

Trade on Secondary Market

+

If you need liquidity before loan maturity, sell your loan portions on our Secondary Market. Or buy additional investments to grow your portfolio.
