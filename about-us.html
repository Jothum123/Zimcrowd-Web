About ZimCrowd - Building Community Finance

We're on a mission to democratize access to fair, transparent financial services. Founded in Zimbabwe, we're building a global platform that puts people first, not profits.

Our Story

How a simple idea about fair finance became a movement for financial inclusion.

The Beginning

Founded in 2020, ZimCrowd started as a response to the financial exclusion many Zimbabweans faced. Traditional banking systems were failing to serve everyday people, leaving communities without access to fair credit.

ü§ù

Community First

We built our platform on the principle that finance should serve people, not the other way around. By leveraging community trust and technology, we created a peer-to-peer lending system that eliminates predatory practices.

üöÄ

Growing Impact

Today, we've facilitated over $2.5M in community lending, helping thousands of Zimbabweans access fair credit. Our AI-powered platform continues to innovate, making financial inclusion a reality for more people every day.

Mission & Values

The principles that guide everything we do.

Our Mission

To democratize access to fair, transparent financial services by building a community-powered lending platform that puts people first, eliminates predatory practices, and fosters economic inclusion worldwide.

Our Values

Transparency: Clear terms, no hidden fees
Community: Building trust through shared responsibility
Innovation: Using technology to solve real problems
Inclusion: Making finance accessible to everyone
