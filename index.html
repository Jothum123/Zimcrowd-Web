How it works

Zimcrowd makes fair, peer-to-peer borrowing simple and transparent. Get the money you need quickly, managing the entire process right from your mobile device.

01

Quick Mobile Setup & Terms

+

Forget endless forms. Create your account and verify your employment directly through the app‚Äîthat's all the "approval" you need. You then set the terms of your loan, including the repayment schedule and your proposed interest rate (which is always optional and voluntary).

02

Get Funded by Your Peers

+

Your request is instantly shared with our community of lenders who believe in supportive, ethical finance. Once your loan is fully funded by fellow members, the money is securely transferred to your linked bank account or mobile wallet.

03

Repayments & Marketplace Flexibility

+

You manage all payments easily through the app, according to the fair terms you established. For Borrowers: You are protected from debt traps and can manage your loan without judgment. For Lenders: We provide a vital safety net. If a lender needs their funds back early, they can sell their portion of the loan to another investor on the Zimcrowd Secondary Market, ensuring liquidity and flexibility for all.

04

Get Smart Guidance with AI Chat

+

Every account comes with Kairo, our integrated AI chat. Use Kairo as your personal financial coach to help you budget for repayments, or as an account assistant to instantly look up your loan status or track investment performance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ZimCrowd's community lending platform

How does ZimCrowd work?

+

ZimCrowd is a peer-to-peer lending platform where community members can lend money directly to other members. Borrowers create loan requests with their terms, and lenders can fund portions of these loans. All transactions are transparent and community-driven.

Is lending safe on ZimCrowd?

+

Yes, ZimCrowd provides Lender Protection that acts as a safety net. If a borrower is unable to repay, the platform ensures lenders are covered. Additionally, all borrowers go through a verification process and provide credit information.

What are the interest rates?

+

Interest rates are completely optional and voluntary on ZimCrowd. Borrowers can choose whether to offer interest, and lenders can decide whether to charge it. This creates fair, flexible terms for everyone involved.

How do I get started?

+

Getting started is simple! Download our app, create your account, and verify your employment. For borrowers, you can immediately start creating loan requests. For lenders, you can begin browsing and funding community loan requests.

What happens if a borrower can't repay?

+

ZimCrowd's Lender Protection covers lenders in case of default. Additionally, our community approach means borrowers are accountable to their local community, creating strong incentives for responsible repayment.

Are there any fees?

+

ZimCrowd is committed to transparency with no hidden fees. The platform operates on a community-driven model, eliminating the overhead costs of traditional financial institutions.
