## 4. BORROWER TERMS

### 4.1 Loan Request Process
1. Submit loan request with required information
2. Platform reviews and assigns ZimScore
3. Loan listed on Primary Market if approved
4. Funding period: 30 days maximum
5. Loan disbursed when fully funded

### 4.2 Borrower Fees

#### Upfront Fees (Deducted Before Disbursement):
- **Service Fee**: 10% of loan amount
- **Insurance Fee**: 3% of loan amount
- **Total Upfront Fees**: 13% of loan amount

#### Monthly Fees (Added to Monthly Payment):
- **Tenure Fee**: 1% of loan amount per month
- **Collection Fee**: 5% of monthly payment

#### Penalty Fees:
- **Late Fee**: 10% of remaining balance (minimum $50)
  - Triggered when payment is 1+ days overdue
  - 95% to Platform, 5% distributed to Lenders

### 4.3 Repayment Obligations
- Monthly payments must be made on or before due date
- Payments include: Principal + Interest + Tenure Fee + Collection Fee
- Late payments incur Late Fee and negatively impact ZimScore
- Early repayment allowed without penalty
- Partial payments not accepted

### 4.4 Default Consequences
- Legal action may be initiated
- ZimScore severely impacted
- Collection fees apply
- Credit bureau reporting
- Account suspension or termination

### 4.5 Borrower Responsibilities
- Provide accurate loan purpose and financial information
- Maintain updated contact information
- Respond to Platform communications within 48 hours
- Use loan funds only for stated purpose
- Report financial hardship immediately

## 5. LENDER/INVESTOR TERMS

### 5.1 Investment Process
1. Browse available loans on Primary Market
2. Review borrower information and risk rating
3. Select investment amount
4. Agree to lender fees and terms
5. Investment processed and funds allocated

### 5.2 Lender Fees

#### Upfront Fees (Deducted Before Investment):
- **Service Fee**: 10% of investment amount
- **Insurance Fee**: 3% of investment amount
- **Total Upfront Fees**: 13% of investment amount

#### Ongoing Fees (Deducted from Monthly Returns):
- **Collection Fee**: 5% of monthly returns received
  - Applies to the investment itself
  - Continues even if investment sold on secondary market

#### Secondary Market Fees:
- **Deal Fee**: 2% of sale price (when selling investment)

### 5.3 Lender Benefits
- **Late Fee Share**: 5% of all late fees collected from borrower
- Interest returns as agreed in loan terms
- Ability to diversify across multiple loans
- Secondary market liquidity option

### 5.4 Investment Risks
- **Default Risk**: Borrower may fail to repay
- **Liquidity Risk**: Secondary market sales not guaranteed
- **Platform Risk**: Platform operations may be disrupted
- **No FDIC Insurance**: Investments not government insured
- **Loss of Capital**: May lose entire investment amount

### 5.5 Lender Responsibilities
- Conduct own due diligence on borrowers
- Understand and accept all risks
- Diversify investments to manage risk
- Monitor investment performance regularly
- Report suspicious activity immediately

## 6. SECONDARY MARKET TERMS

### 6.1 Selling Investments
- Investments can be listed for sale at any time
- Seller sets asking price (can include discount)
- 2% deal fee applies to sale price
- Payment received when buyer completes purchase
- Collection fee continues with new owner

### 6.2 Buying Investments
- Purchase existing investments from other lenders
- Same lender fees apply as primary market
- Inherit remaining loan term and conditions
- 5% collection fee applies to monthly returns
- Receive 5% of future late fees

### 6.3 Secondary Market Rules
- Minimum holding period: None
- Maximum discount: 50%
- Listings expire after 90 days
- Seller can cancel listing anytime before sale
- Platform reserves right to remove listings
