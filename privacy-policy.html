Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last updated: November 5, 2025

Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters: At ZimCrowd, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Date of birth and identification documents
  • Address and employment information
  • Financial information and transaction history
  • Device and usage information

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address and location data
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Device identifiers and cookies

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our lending services
  • To verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • To communicate with you about your account
  • To improve our platform and services
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • To send marketing communications (with consent)

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Other lenders and borrowers (anonymized for matching)
  • Service providers and payment processors
  • Regulatory authorities and law enforcement
  • Credit bureaus and identity verification services
  • Legal counsel when required

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Employee training on data protection

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze website usage
  • Provide personalized experiences
  • Ensure platform security

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than Zimbabwe. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact:

  • Email: privacy@zimcrowd.com
  • Phone: +263 710 467 317
  • Address: 123 Samora Machel Avenue, Harare, Zimbabwe
